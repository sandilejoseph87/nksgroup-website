Our Commitment

People at the Heart of Everything

NKS Group proudly employs over 150 people, the majority of whom are young, black South Africans. Committed to transformation and economic inclusion, we prioritise hiring local youth, support community initiatives, and invest in skills development and training.

🎓

Skills Development

We invest in ongoing training and upskilling of our workforce, building capability and creating long-term career pathways for young South Africans.

🌍

Community Initiatives

We actively support the communities in which we operate, contributing to local development and economic growth through employment and partnerships.

🤝

Transformation & Inclusion

As a 100% black-owned, B-BBEE compliant company, we lead by example in driving meaningful economic transformation across South Africa.

👶

Youth Employment

We prioritise hiring local youth, offering them meaningful employment opportunities and helping to combat unemployment in our communities.
