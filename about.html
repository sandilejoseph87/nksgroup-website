History & Background

From Vision to Industry Leader

Founded in 2008 by visionary entrepreneurs Khangela Kubheka and Paul Silinda, NKS Group (Pty) Ltd was established to strategically outsource non-core operational activities. With deep industry insight and a passion for impactful solutions, the founders built a company that enables clients to focus on their core business while NKS Group manages the rest with precision and professionalism.

Over the years, NKS Group has earned a strong reputation for managing a wide range of non-core operational services, including product reworks, industrial cleaning, and maintenance support.

In 2025, the business embarked on a bold new chapter by diversifying into logistics and warehouse management — providing clients with integrated operational solutions from the factory floor to final delivery.
